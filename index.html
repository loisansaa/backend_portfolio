ABOUT ME

I'm Lois, a passionate developer with a background in Economics and a strong interest in how technology can drive impactful solutions. Over the past year, I've been transitioning into tech, focusing on backend web development while building skills in data science and computer science.

I'm currently working on several backend projects, including building a hospital booking web app where patients can schedule appointments with doctors, manage bookings, and access healthcare services seamlessly. I'm experienced with tools and frameworks like Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and I'm expanding my knowledge into cloud computing and API development.

Alongside my tech journey, my foundation in Economics fuels my interest in the intersection of technology, policy, and innovation. I'm excited about how data and backend systems can power smarter decision-making and create real-world change.

When I'm not coding, I love listening to music, reading, working out, and exploring new interests like anime and gaming.
